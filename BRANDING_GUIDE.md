## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Educational** - We teach, not preach
- **Approachable** - Friendly but professional
- **Scientific** - Research-backed, credible
- **Empowering** - Help users improve themselves

### Writing Style
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and clear
- Avoid jargon (or explain it)
- Be encouraging, not judgmental
- Focus on practical application

### Examples

#### ‚úÖ Good
- "Learn one cognitive bias every day"
- "Recognize and overcome thinking traps"
- "Build better decision-making skills"

#### ‚ùå Avoid
- "Stop being biased" (judgmental)
- "Fix your broken thinking" (negative)
- "Become a genius" (unrealistic)

## Messaging

### Key Messages

1. **Daily Learning**
   - One bias per day
   - Bite-sized, manageable content
   - Build knowledge gradually

2. **Research-Backed**
   - 50 cognitive biases
   - Scientific sources
   - Evidence-based strategies

3. **Practical Application**
   - Real-world examples
   - Actionable counter-strategies
   - Immediate value

4. **Privacy-First**
   - All data stored locally
   - No tracking or ads
   - Your learning, your data

### Elevator Pitch
